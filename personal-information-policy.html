Personal Information Policy

Personal Information Protection Policy

At Canadian Sport, your privacy is paramount. This Personal Information Protection Policy describes how we collect, use and protect your personal information, while ensuring compliance with applicable privacy laws.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: This includes your name, email address, phone number and any other information you provide when booking an event or subscribing to our newsletter.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with our website, including your IP address, browser type and pages visited.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to improve your browsing experience, remembering your preferences and improving our services.

How We Use Your Information

Your personal information may be used for the following purposes:

  • To facilitate your event bookings and provide customer support.
  • To personalize your experience and communicate relevant offers and updates.
  • To improve our website and services based on user feedback and interactions.

Data Security

We implement strict security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encryption of sensitive data.
  • Regular security audits to identify and address vulnerabilities.
  • Restricted access to personal information limited to authorized personnel only.

Your Rights

As a user, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request corrections for any inaccuracies or incomplete information.
  • Request deletion of your personal information in accordance with applicable laws.

Commitment to Your Privacy

At Canadian Sport, we are committed to never selling or renting your personal information to third parties. Your data will only be shared with trusted partners to facilitate our services, and this will be done under strict confidentiality agreements.

Policy Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Any significant changes will be communicated to you by email or through notifications on our site.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this policy, please do not hesitate to contact us.

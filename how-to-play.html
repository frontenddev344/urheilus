How to Play

Finland Sport, your gateway to the exciting world of fantasy sports! Whether you're a seasoned veteran or a newcomer, our step-by-step guide will help you navigate our platform with ease. From registration and team selection to managing your lineup and competing for the championship, we have everything you need. Dive in, engage with our dynamic community and enjoy the thrill of fantasy sports like never before. Let's get started!

1

Sign up and create an account

  • Visit the website: Go to Finland Sport and sign up.
  • Register: Fill in your details, including your name, email and password.
  • Confirm email: Check your email for a confirmation link and click it to activate your account.
2

Choose your sport

  • Login: use your credentials to log into your account.
  • Select a sport: choose from football, soccer, basketball or hockey.
3

Join or create a league

  • Join a league: browse available leagues and join one that matches your interests.
  • Create a league: if you prefer, create your own league. Set up the rules, the scoring system and invite friends or make it public.
4

Draft your team

  • Draft date: Check the draft date and time set by your league.
  • Preparation: research players, statistics and strategies before the draft.
  • Draft day: participate in the live draft to select your players, or set up an auto-draft if you cannot attend.
5

Manage your team

  • Set lineups: before each game week, set your starting lineup. Make sure to consider matchups and player performances.
  • Make trades: trade players with other managers to improve your team.
  • Waiver wire: pick up free agents to replace underperforming players or cover injuries.
6

Follow the games

  • Watch live: follow real games to see your players' performances.
  • Score tracking: Check your team's performance and ranking within your league on Finland Sport.
7

Compete and win

  • Weekly matchups: face other teams in your league each week.
  • Playoffs: If your league has playoffs, work to qualify and compete for the championship.
  • Prizes: win bragging rights, prizes or simply enjoy the fun and excitement of fantasy sports.
8

Stay engaged

  • Community: join community forums to discuss strategies, trades and game results.
  • Updates: stay informed with news, tips and expert advice through our blog and newsletters.
9

Renew or start new leagues

  • End of season: at the end of the season, you can choose to renew your league or start fresh for the next season.
  • Invite friends: encourage your friends to join and grow your fantasy sports community.
  • Enjoy your fantasy sports experience with Finland Sport and may the best team win!
